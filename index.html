About Me

Hi, I'm Bobby Hackett. I'm getting my MS in physics at the University of Washington (UW). Prior to joining UW, I graduated from Arizona State University with a BS in Physics and a minor in Astrophysics.

When I'm not studying, I enjoy playing music, painting, and taking walks with my wife and our chihuahua.
